Shipping Information

Everything you need to know about sending packages with us.

📋 Shipping Guidelines

Standard Shipping

Standard shipping typically takes 5-10 business days for domestic deliveries and 10-30 business days for international deliveries. This is our most economical shipping option.

Express Shipping

Express shipping delivers your packages in 1-3 business days domestically and 3-10 business days internationally. Ideal for time-sensitive deliveries.

Package Requirements

  • Maximum weight: 30 kg
  • Maximum dimensions: 120cm x 60cm x 60cm
  • Proper packaging required to prevent damage
  • Accurate address formatting is essential

Prohibited Items

  • Hazardous materials
  • Perishable goods
  • Illegal substances
  • Live animals
  • Cash and valuable documents (use specialized services)

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate shipping costs?

Shipping costs are calculated based on package weight, dimensions, origin, destination, and shipping type. Use our order form or chat with our AI assistant for a quote.

Can I change the delivery address after shipping?

Address changes may be possible before delivery. Contact our support team as soon as possible with your tracking number.

What happens if my package is damaged?

Report damaged packages within 48 hours of delivery. Take photos of the damaged package and its contents. Our support team will guide you through the claims process.

Do you offer pickup services?

Yes, we offer scheduled pickups for business and bulk shipments. Contact our sales team for pricing.
