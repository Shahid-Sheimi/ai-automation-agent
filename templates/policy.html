Lost Package Policy

Learn what to do if your package is lost or delayed.

🚨 Immediate Steps if Your Package is Lost

  1. Check tracking status: Use our tracking tool to see the latest updates. Sometimes packages show as "delayed" rather than lost.
  2. Wait 48 hours: Sometimes packages show tracking delays due to weather, customs, or carrier issues.
  3. Chat with our AI assistant: Get personalized guidance on the next steps based on your specific situation.
  4. File a formal claim: If the package is confirmed lost, you'll need to file a claim with all supporting documents.

📋 Required Documents

  • Proof of purchase / invoice
  • Original tracking number
  • Photo of package (if available)
  • Government-issued ID

⏱️ Claim Timeline

  • Standard: Reviewed within 5-10 business days
  • Express: Reviewed within 2-5 business days
  • International: Reviewed within 10-20 business days

💰 Compensation Policy

Compensation amounts depend on the declared value and service type. For detailed information, chat with our AI assistant about your specific shipment.
